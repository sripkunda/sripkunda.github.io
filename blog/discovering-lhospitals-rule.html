Remark: Spelling and Pronunciation

Consider:

  • Lo as in Low,
  • pi as in Pinch,
  • Tal as in Talisman.

The final pronunciation in this form makes Lo + pi + tal.

It is also spelled "L'Hôpital's rule," but we will use L'hospital's rule in this writing because it is easier to type.
