Vector Spaces and Matrices

Vector Spaces are the subject of linear algebra (LA). In most engineering first courses in LA, the beautiful generalization that vector spaces provide for these arbitrary objects called "vectors" is overlooked. This article tries to build upon a less theoretical background of linear algebra to discuss vector spaces and how they shape the ideas of LA as well as the already familiar concept of a matrix.

Although this article discusses only linear algebra, my ultimate goal with this article is to take a commonly studied topic that does not rely too much on other branches of mathematics (at least initially) and introduce the pure math side of it. The hope is that this article will encourage scientists and engineers to step into the pure mathematics rabbithole.
