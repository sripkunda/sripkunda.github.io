Projects

Here are some of my favorite projects. Most of them are open sourced and can be found on my github. If the link does not work, it probably means I am not done working on it.

Candl

A tiny, pedagogical neural network library with a pytorch-like API and tensor-valued autodiff.

Accent.js

An open source web development framework made in less than 10kb.

A tool for businesses to give and receive feedback from their customers, used by 100+ businesses to collect and manage their customer feedback.
